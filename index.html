The hardships that come with raising an autistic child can be both challenging and rewarding! Join Colorfull Minds Center on its mission to celebrate your child’s strengths and overcome challenging situations with the help of highly qualified BCBA’s (Board Certified Behavior Analyst) and ABA therapists!
Our team is passionate about serving and making a lasting impact on your child’s life. Colorfull Minds Center provides exceptional care and attention to families in the surrounding ABA Therapy is provided in the comfort of your home, school or community where your child can learn in their natural environment.
Our Clinicians work with you in delivering the support and tools your child deserves to reach their full potential. ABA therapy is effective at improving various skills such as communication, social-emotional, daily living, executive functions, academics, and many more! Learning should be colorful and adaptable to your child’s needs.

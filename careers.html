Registered Behavior Technician (RBT)

We are looking for ABA therapists to provide intensive one to one therapy for children with autism and other learning disabilities. ABA therapy will be conducted in-home, in schools, and at the center. If interested in becoming a RBT, contact us!

Qualifcations:

Must have a love for working with children, be 18 years old, graduated high school, and be able to pass a background test.

Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA)

We are looking for experienced BCBAs who want to work with individuals with developmental disabilities in a range of settings. You must be able to:

  • Provide training for new therapists
  • Create curriculum programs and procedures
  • Develop and oversee individualized treatment plans based on the principles of ABA
  • Conduct behavioral assessments
  • Analyze data and make data-based program changes
  • Write reports documenting client’s progress to submit to funding sources
  • Conduct parent trainings
  • Lead team meetings
  • Be able to multitask and be a part of a collaborative team!

Qualifcations:

Must be registered as a BCBA and in good standing with the BACB! Must be licensed in the state of Mississippi and be able to pass a background test.

Board Certified Assistant Behavior Analyst: (BCaBA)

Under the supervision of a Board-Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA), the Board Certified Assistant Behavior Analyst (BCaBA) provides supervision and acts as a role model for the team.

  • Proficient with all duties of an ABA Therapist
  • Creates and updates client programming and behavior intervention plans under guidance of the BCBA
  • Provides ongoing feedback, training, and supervision to RBTs
  • Assists BCBA with functional analyses and skills/developmental assessments
  • Creates data sheets to monitor progress and behaviors as needed
  • Consistently works to improve quality of therapy by seizing opportunities to train and teach in all environments
  • Attends and actively participates in parent team meetings, IEP meetings, Therapist meetings, trainings and other meetings as requested
  • Monitors effectiveness of staff training activities and materials and provides feedback to Administration as needed

Qualifcations:

Must be registered as a BCaBA and in good standing with the BACB! Must be licensed in the state of Mississippi and be able to pass a background test.

About Us

We are approved in providing applied behavior analysis services to children with Autism, ADHD, developmental disabilities. Colorfull Minds Center, was founded by two committed individuals with a passion for helping children with special needs reach their full potential and improve their quality of life. Our mission is to increase our clients’ social and functional skills and reduce problem behaviors by implementing the research-based strategies of Applied Behavior Analysis. Colorfull Minds Center is a private agency experienced in delivering the highest quality therapeutic services to children and their families. At CMC we provide applied behavior analysis by highly trained and skilled staff, such as Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA), Board Certified Assistant Behavior Analyst (BCaBA), and Registered Behavior Technicians (RBT).

Our Goal

Our goal is to serve our clients by tailoring individualized behavioral programs that promote independence and use best practices to facilitate reaching their highest potential.
